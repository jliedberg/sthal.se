— Inspiration —

Whether a celebration, decoration or constellation, we want our handicrafts to fit in, no matter the occasion. As designers and stylists with a passionate interest to socialize and dine, we were missing dishes and bowls with shapes, colors and patterns out of the ordinary. A design which would make the food justice and create the right feel for the occasion.
